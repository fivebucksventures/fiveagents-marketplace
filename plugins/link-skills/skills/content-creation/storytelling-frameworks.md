# Storytelling Frameworks

## AIDA

**Best for:** Landing pages, Google/LinkedIn ads, email subject lines + preview text

**Structure:**
- **Attention** — Grab the reader's attention with a bold headline that names their pain or desire
- **Interest** — Build interest by expanding on the problem and showing you understand their world
- **Desire** — Create desire by painting the "after" picture and showing how fivebucks.ai gets them there
- **Action** — Direct them to a specific, low-friction next step (free trial, demo, compare plans)

**Template:**
```
[Attention] Headline: [Pain point or desired outcome — be specific]
[Interest] You're spending X hours doing Y manually, while your competitors...
[Desire] With fivebucks.ai, you can [outcome] in [timeframe] — without [frustration].
[Action] Start your 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
```

**fivebucks.ai example (SEO Professional persona):**
```
Attention: Your competitors are already showing up in ChatGPT. Are you?
Interest: As AI search grows, traditional SEO alone is no longer enough.
  Professionals tracking only Google rankings are missing half the picture.
Desire: fivebucks.ai tracks your rankings across both Google SERP and AI Search —
  ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity — so you see the complete visibility picture
  and optimize for where search is heading, not just where it's been.
Action: Start your 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
```

## PAS

**Best for:** Email body copy, landing page body sections, long-form blog intros

**Structure:**
- **Problem** — Name the specific problem the persona faces (use their language)
- **Agitate** — Make the problem feel more urgent by expanding its cost (time, money, opportunity)
- **Solution** — Introduce fivebucks.ai as the clear answer, backed by specific features

**Template:**
```
[Problem] You're using [X tools] to do what should be one workflow.
[Agitate] Every hour switching between tools is an hour not spent on growth.
  And with fragmented data, you're making decisions with an incomplete picture.
[Solution] fivebucks.ai combines [feature 1], [feature 2], and [feature 3]
  in one platform — so you can [outcome] without [pain].
```

**fivebucks.ai example (Content Marketing Manager persona):**
```
Problem: You have SEMrush for research, Jasper for writing, WordPress for publishing,
  and Google Analytics for reporting. That's four tabs, four logins, four bills.

Agitate: The context-switching alone costs you hours each week. And when the data
  doesn't sync across tools, you end up publishing content based on incomplete insights —
  content that competes with itself, misses keyword opportunities, or just doesn't rank.

Solution: fivebucks.ai combines keyword research, AI content creation, multi-platform
  publishing, and SERP + AI Search monitoring in one workflow. Research a keyword,
  generate an optimized article, publish to WordPress and LinkedIn, and track
  performance — all from one dashboard.
```

## BAB

**Best for:** Social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X), email subject lines, short ad copy

**Structure:**
- **Before** — Describe the painful "before" state (present reality without fivebucks.ai)
- **After** — Paint the desirable "after" state (life with fivebucks.ai)
- **Bridge** — Show how fivebucks.ai is the bridge from before to after

**Template:**
```
[Before] Right now, you're [doing painful thing manually or with multiple tools].
[After] Imagine [the outcome they want] — [specific, concrete result].
[Bridge] fivebucks.ai [specific capability] makes that possible. [CTA]
```

**fivebucks.ai example (Solopreneur/David persona):**
```
Before: Right now, you're cobbling together free SEO tools, ChatGPT, and a
  WordPress plugin to compete with companies 10x your size.

After: Imagine having a site diagnostic, keyword plan, ready-to-publish article,
  and lead capture all set up — in an afternoon.

Bridge: fivebucks.ai Basic gives solo founders everything they need for $39/month.
  Start free for 14 days.
```

## Problem-Solution

**Best for:** Blog posts, comparison pages, SEO articles, feature explainers

**Structure:**
- **Problem statement** — Define the problem clearly and objectively (2-3 sentences)
- **Why it matters** — Quantify or qualify the cost of not solving it
- **Existing approaches and why they fall short** — Acknowledge alternatives honestly
- **The fivebucks.ai solution** — Introduce the feature/capability that solves it
- **How it works** — Brief step-by-step or description (2-4 steps)
- **Outcome** — What the reader achieves after using it

**Template:**
```
# [Problem-framing headline]

[Problem] [Persona] faces [specific challenge] because [root cause].

[Why it matters] This means [measurable consequence — time lost, opportunities missed, cost].

[Existing approaches] Most [personas] try to solve this with [tool/approach], but [limitation].

[Solution] fivebucks.ai [feature name] solves this by [mechanism].

[How it works]
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

[Outcome] The result: [specific, concrete outcome for this persona].
```
