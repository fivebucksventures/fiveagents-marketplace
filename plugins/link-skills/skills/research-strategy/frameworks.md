# Research & Strategy Frameworks

## ICP framework

**Purpose:** Define exactly who we're targeting before writing a single word of copy.

**ICP template:**

```markdown
### Ideal Customer Profile: [Persona Name]

**Role:** [Job title(s)]
**Seniority:** [e.g., Manager, Director, Founder]
**Company size:** [e.g., 5-50 employees / 1 person]
**Industry:** [e.g., B2B SaaS, digital marketing agency, e-commerce]
**Geography:** [e.g., US/UK/AU, Indonesia (ID)]

**Primary goal:** [What they are trying to achieve at work]
**Biggest blocker:** [What's currently stopping them from achieving it]

**Current tools:** [What they use today — reference context/audience.md]
**Trigger event:** [What causes them to start looking for a new solution]

**Plan tier fit:** Basic | Professional | Enterprise
**Primary CTA:** Free trial | Demo | Compare plans | Contact sales
```

## Positioning framework

**Purpose:** Define fivebucks.ai' unique position in the market for a specific campaign or segment.

**Positioning statement template:**
```
For [target persona],
who [pain point or unmet need],
fivebucks.ai is the [category definition]
that [key benefit/differentiator],
unlike [named competitor or alternative],
which [limitation of the alternative].
```

**Filled example (vs. SEMrush, for Marcus):**
```
For SEO professionals managing large portfolios,
who need to optimize for AI Search in addition to traditional Google rankings,
fivebucks.ai is the only all-in-one growth platform
that tracks SERP rankings AND AI citations (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) in one dashboard,
unlike SEMrush,
which provides analysis but no content execution, publishing, or AI Search tracking.
```

**5 Positioning angles to choose from (pick 1-2 per campaign):**

| Angle | Use when | Key message |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| AI Search leadership | Targeting SEO-savvy personas | "The only platform optimizing for Google AND AI Search" |
| Traffic-to-leads bridge | Targeting growth/sales-aligned personas | "Generate traffic AND leads — one workflow, not two" |
| Execution over analysis | Targeting personas frustrated by insight-only tools | "fivebucks.ai doesn't just tell you what to do — it does it with you" |
| Pricing transparency | Targeting cost-conscious buyers | "Replace $500+/month in tools with one $39-$350 platform" |
| Speed to value | Targeting personas burned by complex platforms | "Start in hours. See results in days. No implementation partner needed." |

## Messaging framework

**Purpose:** Build 3-5 core messages for a campaign, each tied to a proof point and objection.

**Message template (repeat for each message):**
```markdown
### Message [N]: [Short label]

**Claim:** [What fivebucks.ai does for this persona — 1 sentence]
**Proof point:** [Specific feature or data from context/product.md]
**Competitive angle:** [How this beats the named alternative — from context/competitors.md]
**Objection handled:** [Which objection from context/audience.md this addresses]
**Approved phrase:** [Relevant phrase from `brands/{brand}/brand.md`, if applicable]
```

**Filled example for Marcus (SEO Professional campaign):**

```markdown
### Message 1: AI Search Visibility

Claim: fivebucks.ai is the only platform that tracks both Google SERP rankings
  and AI search citations in a single dashboard.
Proof point: Monitor & Improve Performance feature — tracks ChatGPT citations,
  AI mentions, and SERP positions (1-3, 4-10, 11-20) with monthly refresh cycles.
Competitive angle: SEMrush and Ahrefs track Google rankings only. Neither has
  AI Search monitoring.
Objection handled: "What about AI search optimization — is it proven?" →
  Cite early mover advantage and AI citation tracking metrics.
Approved phrase: "Optimize for Google & AI Search"

### Message 2: End-to-End Execution

Claim: fivebucks.ai doesn't just show you what to fix — it helps you fix it,
  from content creation to publishing to tracking.
Proof point: Full Traffic Generation workflow: Site Diagnostics → Keyword Research
  → AI Content Generation (SEO Score 95/100, AI Ready Score 88/100) → Multi-platform
  Publishing → Performance Monitoring.
Competitive angle: SEMrush provides analysis only. Separate tools needed for
  content and publishing. fivebucks.ai is one workflow.
Objection handled: "How is this different from ChatGPT + SEMrush?" →
  Purpose-built, integrated data, automated publishing.
Approved phrase: "From site diagnostics to content creation"
```

**CTA language rules:**
- Always pair trial CTAs with "No credit card required"

- For demo CTAs: "Book a 20-minute demo" (concrete time reduces friction)
- For pricing page: "Compare all plans" (not "See pricing" — implies comparison value)
